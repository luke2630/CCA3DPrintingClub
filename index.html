ABOUT US

What we do

Our group members use 3D printers to print the face-shield for hospitals. The donation will be all used to buy the required materials, such as films and filaments. After members finished the weekly printing plan, all the face-shields would be sent out from a constant spot. We guaranteed you that our face shields will be free and all for public benefical
aboutus

Who we are

We are students from an educational organization named IvyMax. Our group members are from middle schools and high schools in San Diego. For now, there are 15 students working on this project in our group, and we are still expanding our team.
